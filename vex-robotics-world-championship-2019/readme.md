# VEX Robotics World Championship 2019

## Overview
This project documents the design and development of a competition robot for the VEX Robotics World Championship 2019.

## Contributions
- Mechanical design and robot construction
- System integration and iterative prototyping
- Team leadership and coordination

## Results
- Participated in the VEX Robotics World Championship (Louisville, USA)
- Awarded the Energy Award among ~8000 teams from 40 countries

## Technologies
- VEX Robotics Platform
- Mechanical design
- Rapid prototyping
